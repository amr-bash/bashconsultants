Notes from the field for people who run small and medium businesses (SMBs) and the technology inside them. We write about the systems that keep a company running — Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP), accounting, cloud, data, and the AI now threading through all of it — in plain English, with the costs and trade-offs left in.

Four sections, four registers: [corporate strategy](/news/corp/) for owners and chief financial officers (CFOs), [business systems](/news/erp/) told the way they actually feel from the back office, [longer-form essays](/news/muses/) on how technology and work collide, and [hands-on technical guides](/news/tech/) for the person who has to make it run.

If a piece raises a question about your own systems, [our services page](/services/) is the place to start.
